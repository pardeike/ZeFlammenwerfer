Sometimes a normal gun is too polite.

Ze Flammenwerfer adds a fuel-fed flamethrower to RimWorld 1.6. It is loud, messy, dangerous, and very good at making a corridor someone else's problem.

What it adds:

- a flamethrower weapon
- fuel use
- visible tank and pipe equipment
- custom flame and smoke effects
- enough fire to make positioning matter

Keep it fueled, keep your colonists out of the line of fire, and try not to solve every problem by turning the map orange.
