# Hans, Get Ze Flammenwerfer!

Sometimes a normal gun is too polite.

Ze Flammenwerfer adds a proper flamethrower to RimWorld 1.6: a loud, hungry, fuel-fed weapon that paints the battlefield with fire, smoke, and bad decisions. It is made for moments where you want a corridor denied, an infestation regretted, or a wooden wall to become someone else's problem.

The weapon has its own fuel system, visible tank and pipe equipment, custom flame and smoke effects, and enough heat to make positioning matter. Keep it fueled, keep your colonists clear of the line of fire, and try not to solve every problem by turning the map orange.

Requires Harmony.
